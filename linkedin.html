How to use this template

square · 1:1 · 1080px export

1 · Pick a layout. A — a one-line insight hook. B — a single big Fraunces-italic stat. C — a customer quote. D — an announcement with a CTA button.
2 · Edit in place. Click Edit text: on, then click any field and type. Keep one — and only one — Fraunces-italic guest phrase per headline (wrap it in <span class="fa">).
3 · Stay on-brand. Square corners only, navy-tinted surfaces (never pure #fff/#000), amber lives in the chip and logo alone, teal carries the rule. Crosshair marks are marketing-only — fine here.
4 · Export. Switch to Actual size · 1080px and screenshot the canvas (or print-to-PDF, then crop to the square). Upload to LinkedIn as a single-image post.
