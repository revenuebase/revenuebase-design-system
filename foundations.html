07 — Calls I made

Decisions to confirm

These are the judgment calls baked into the foundation. Flag any you'd change.

Square corners + crosshairs

0px on buttons, cards, inputs, tags and panels (only the nav rounds, 8px). Crosshair marks sit at the corners of buttons & framed panels — a real site element, not selection handles. Don't soften either.

The primary button is quiet

Marketing primary CTA = muted navy-150 #c1cfe0 fill with navy-800 text and a hairline border — not a saturated color. Secondary is an outline. Teal fills are for in-app actions only.

Navy is primary, amber is accent

Navy dominates UI & text; amber/gold is reserved for the logo mark and brand moments — never as a button or large fill. Teal carries action.

Two text ramps

Marketing text runs on navy (brand-saturated); product text runs on the neutral slate ramp for readability, with navy reserved for links/prices.

Both modes are tinted

Light = navy-50, dark = navy-900. Deliberately not pure #fff / #000, matching the live site.

Texture stays whisper-quiet

Noise rides at 4% over the canvas; the grid is reserved for panels, not full pages. Both are real site assets, saved locally and theme-swapped.

Warning color is lightly used

Orange/amber warning appears in the product screens as an inline alert banner. It is documented in the palette but is not a primary UI color.
